THE CONCEPT

During this second year we developed a collective project called MIRALLS DEL DEMÃ€. Our team came together during the Interaction and Prototyping Seminar to create an installation that would bridge technology and consciousness about the future. The conceptual phase was particularly engaging as we explored ways to transform individual voices into collective visual experiences. We wanted to create more than just an interactive piece trying to develop a platform that would reflect humanity's relationship with its future. Through many discussions and iterations, we settled on the idea of using AI to transform spoken thoughts into dynamic visualizations, making abstract concerns about our planet's future tangible and immediate.

PROTOTYPING

The prototyping process brought its own set of challenges. Our team worked to integrate various technologies (proximity sensors, microphones, and AI models) into a cohesive interaction. We invcested a lot of time testing different approaches to ensure the technology would serve our concept rather than overshadow it. The process of fine tunning the AI to interpret speech into meaningful(and interesting) visual patterns was particularly complexas well as it's web interaction, but it was awesome to see how each technical solution started coming together shaping the whole system.

The final installation and testing phase, where everything came together, was the important part. Watching the LED panel come to life and the interactions unfold was incredibly rewarding. We positioned the microphoneto create an inviting space for visitors to share their thoughts, and the way the AI transforms these words into visual projections exceeded our expectations (spetially with children!!!). The installation tryed to highlight critical issues like housing, urbanization, future transportation... while maintaining an artistic and engaging presence. What makes me most proud is how our collective work succeeded in creating a space that not only raises awareness about environmental challenges but also inspires hope and action for a sustainable future.
