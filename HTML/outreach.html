SLOW FURNITURE THESIS PUBLISHED (2023)

Finally, after a lot of work, I'm proud to say that my thesis project 'Slow Furniture: Disseny d'un moble evolutiu, durador i emocional' has been published at the repository of the Universitat Politècnica de València. The work proposes a solution to the growing amount of furniture discarded after its single life cycle, especially those intended for use by children. The project investigates the creation of an evolving piece of furniture capable of changing its typology, accompanying children during their growth and even being kept until adulthood. Based on the principles of the Circular Economy, the proposal proposes a product that can be transformed, extending its useful life in different cycles and thus maintaining not only its physical value but also all the resources used during its manufacture. Furthermore, it is designed to create an emotional link between the person and the object that motivates the individual to give second and third lives to the pieces of furniture, thus delaying as much as possible or even avoiding their arrival in the landfill.

PREMIS ARTESANIA CATEGORIA
'NOUS TALENTS' (2022)

The Valencian Community Craft Center works to promote and support local craftsmanship, helping artisanal businesses grow and stay competitive. They run various programs to encourage innovation, adapt to market trends, and support sustainable development. As part of their efforts, they hold the Valencian Community Craft Awards to recognize and celebrate the skills and achievements of local artisans. These awards also highlight the best projects from universities and art and design schools in the region. I was thrilled to be a finalist in the "Nous Talents" category, with my project "Gresal" showcased in the Premis d'Artesania Comunitat Valenciana 2022 exhibition. This recognition shows my dedication to craftsmanship and design, placing my work among the most promising projects in the Valencian Community.
